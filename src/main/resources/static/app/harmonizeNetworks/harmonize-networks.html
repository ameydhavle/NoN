Sanitize & Envelope Pattern

Use the Envelope data pattern to encapsulate data. Simply put, the envelope pattern wraps your data in either XML or JSON like so:
<envelope>
 </headers>
 </triples>
 <content> your data goes here </content>
</envelope>
